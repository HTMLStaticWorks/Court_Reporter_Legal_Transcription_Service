Transcription Options

Select the transcript format that integrates seamlessly with your firm's litigation software and review processes.

Verbatim Transcripts

Word-for-word recording capturing every utterance, including false starts, stutters, and non-verbal utterances. Essential for witness impeachment.

Cleaned / Edited Transcripts

Verbatim content with false starts and stutters removed for readability. Ideal for corporate meetings and internal investigations.

Summary Transcripts

Condensed summaries of key topics and decisions. Often used for lengthy board meetings or multi-day symposiums where only key takeaways are required.
