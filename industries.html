Domain-Specific Experts

Generic transcriptionists often struggle with industry-specific terminology. We route your case files to certified specialists who understand the complex vocabulary of your field.

  • Medical-Legal Transcriptionists: Decipher complex anatomical terms, pharmaceutical names, and surgical terminology accurately.
  • IP & Tech Jargon Specialists: Native speakers who understand engineering, computer science, and chemical nomenclature.
  • Financial Legal Experts: Precise transcription of mergers, acquisitions, SEC filings, and corporate audits.

Direct Integration with Legal Technology

Our transcripts are formatted to load directly into the industry-standard software and e-discovery tools used by top AmLaw firms and corporate legal departments.

  • Relativity & Concordance Ready: ASCII format files and synchronized videos configured for direct ingestion.
  • Hyperlinked Exhibits: PDF transcripts with embedded, secure links to the associated digital evidence.
  • E-Transcripts (.PTX): Digital signatures, word index tables, and compressed format bundles.
