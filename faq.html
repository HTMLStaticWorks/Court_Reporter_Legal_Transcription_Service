Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our enterprise services and security protocols.

Are your digital pathways truly secure for highly sensitive evidence?

Yes. We utilize military-grade 256-bit AES encryption both in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II compliant and housed in high-security, geographic-redundant data centers.

What is the turnaround time for a standard deposition?

Our standard turnaround time is 5 to 7 business days. However, we offer guaranteed expedited options including 48-hour, 24-hour, and same-day delivery upon request.

Do you provide certified video synchronization?

Absolutely. We provide high-definition digital video that is meticulously synchronized with the certified text transcript, deliverable in LEF, CMS, and standard MP4/PDF bundles.

Can we integrate your services with Relativity?

Yes. Our output formats are natively compatible with Relativity, Summation, Concordance, and all major litigation support software suites.
