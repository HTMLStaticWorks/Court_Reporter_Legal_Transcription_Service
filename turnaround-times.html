Real-Time Scoping Workflow

To guarantee Same Day and 24-Hour turnaround times without compromising accuracy, we use a synchronized real-time scoping pipeline. This team-based approach ensures immediate verification at every step.

  • Realtime Streaming: Steno writers broadcast machine strokes over a secure cloud portal for immediate remote access.
  • Concurrently Scoped: Dedicated scopists edit spelling, formatting, and speaker names during the live proceeding.
  • Multi-Stage QC Review: A senior legal editor runs a secondary spelling and citation audit prior to final certification.

Turnaround FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Understand how turnaround policies operate and how to configure schedules for your team.

How are business days counted?

Business days are counted starting the day after the proceeding concludes, assuming files/audio are logged by 5:00 PM. Submissions logged after 5:00 PM will be processed starting the next business day.

What is a rough draft?

A rough draft is an uncertified, unedited transcript in ASCII text format, provided for immediate outline reference. It is not a legal document and should not be cited in motions or courtroom proceedings.

Can I upgrade turnaround times mid-case?

Yes. If discovery needs change, you can contact your scheduling coordinator to expedite the transcription queue. Fees will be adjusted based on the new delivery tier requested.

Do you handle multi-day proceedings?

Absolutely. Multi-day hearings can be configured for rolling daily deliveries (Same Day / 24-Hour) so that your trial team receives transcripts of each day's testimonies prior to the next morning's session.
