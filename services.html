Our Three-Step Quality Control Process

To achieve a verified 99.9% accuracy rating, every legal transcript goes through our multi-phase editing pipeline. This rigorous checking system guarantees error-free legal documentation.

  • Step 1: Certified Audio Capture Verbatim transcription mapping live audio records to digital shorthand notes.
  • Step 2: Concurrent Scoping Immediate transcript editing and auditing of technical terminology by domain experts.
  • Step 3: Final QC Proofread Complete line-by-line validation by a senior editor before digital signing and delivery.
