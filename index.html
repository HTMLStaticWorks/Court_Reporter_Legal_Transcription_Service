How It Works

From Case File to Certified Transcript

A seamless, secure five-step process designed to eliminate friction and deliver flawless documentation under the tightest deadlines.

Start a Case

1 — Secure File Submission

Upload case materials (audio, video, exhibits) via our 256-bit encrypted portal — files up to 50GB supported.

2 — Reporter Matching

Our dispatch system instantly routes your case to the certified reporter best matched for your jurisdiction and subject matter.

3 — Transcription & Scoping

The reporter produces the verbatim record. A dedicated scopist reviews against witness spellings, technical terms, and exhibits.

4 — QC Proofreading

A senior proofreader performs a final accuracy pass against the audio, ensuring 99.9% word-for-word fidelity before certification.

5 — Secure Delivery

Certified transcripts are deposited directly into your firm's secure dashboard, available in all required formats (.ptx, .pdf, .lef, ASCII).
